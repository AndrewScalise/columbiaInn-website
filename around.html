Columbia is ranked among the top cities to live in in Missouri. With great education facilities, safety, and that small town feel it has a bit of everything. The population is about at 115,000 people making it the largest city in Mid Missouri.

Columbia is diverse and has a unique set of cultural diversity and celebrates both music and film. Going to the blue note can mean a great night of enjoying music. While visiting the RagTag Cinema can be a nice chill night enjoying the movies. Also a unique celebration of film happens during True/False film festivals where spectators become critics for which films they enjoyed or didn't.
