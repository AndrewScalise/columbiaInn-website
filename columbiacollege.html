Columbia College

The hotel has a long history of relationship with Columbia College. That's how the hotel got its name. In, fact many of our customers are parents of Columbia College who visit their children on campus, or alumni of the college. We are a strong sponsor of Cougar Teams since it was founded in 1996.

Columbia College is a well rounded college that provides courses for a fair cost. Courses are offered both on campus, and online so that even long distance learners have an opportunity to learn from one of the best colleges in the state. The athletics department has also grown, and even features a progressive sport such as online-gaming.
